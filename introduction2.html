ETHIOPIA
01

Ethiopian & Western:
An Oxymoron

I grew up in Ethiopia for six years before
moving to America. This makes me too Western
for the Ethiopians and too Ethiopian for the
Westerners. In my community, this is a
paradox that many young immigrants face.

02

We Moved to Seattle
for a Better Life

However, culture constantly
surrounds my siblings and I at home.
Our parents instilled in us a strong
sense of commitment to culture
and a fond attachment to home. To this day,
I still know how to perform the art of making
a traditional Ethiopian coffee ceremony.

03

In Summary:
My Heart Will
Always be in Ethiopia
