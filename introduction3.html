The Emerald City

It really is as green and rainy as you've
probably heard. Living in The Emerald City
has taught me how to detect rain
from the wind. Fun fact: locals and natives
wear jackets in the rain so tourists
with umbrellas really stand out.

02

Let's Go Tomorrow

Living here makes locals not want to
go out. If I'm in Seattle, my preferred Friday
night is usually Netflix because its so rainy
outside and comfortable inside. This is
the general consensous unless its May
to August.
